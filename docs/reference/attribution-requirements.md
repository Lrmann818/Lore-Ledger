_Phase 4 work — required before public release_

An in-app attribution/credits page must be created before Lore Ledger is released publicly. This page must display the CC-BY-4.0 attribution statement from `LEGAL.md`:

> This work includes material taken from the System Reference Document 5.1 ("SRD 5.1") by Wizards of the Coast LLC and available at https://dnd.wizards.com/resources/systems-reference-document. The SRD 5.1 is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License available at https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode.

The statement in `LEGAL.md` is the canonical source. Keep both in sync.

## Placement

The attribution notice should appear in a location accessible to users, such as:

- an About / Credits / Legal section within the Data & Settings modal
- a dedicated Credits panel or screen

## This is a hard requirement

Distributing SRD-derived builtin content without this attribution in place does not satisfy the CC-BY-4.0 license terms. Do not skip or defer past the Phase 4 milestone.
